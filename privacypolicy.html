PythoMy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

TechnoRock (a subsidiary of Team Turtle Hack) operates PythoMy. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the PythoMy app and website.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  1. What personal information we collect, how it is used, and with whom it may be shared.
  2. Your choices regarding the use of your data.
  3. The security measures in place to protect your information.
  4. How you can update or delete your information.

By using PythoMy, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.


Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We collect information that you voluntarily provide during registration, profile updates, app interactions, or feedback submissions. This information helps us deliver and improve our services. The collected data includes, but is not limited to:

Publicly Visible Information
  1. Full Name: Your first and last name.
  2. E-mail Address: Used for account verification and updates.
  3. Username Your alias within the app.
  4. Role: Your role in the app community (e.g., Novice or Expert).
  5. Forums and Comments: Your posts and interactions within lessons/topics and community forums.
  6. Leaderboard Scores and Ranks: Your achievements in quizzes and rankings.
  7. Appointments, Seminars and Activities: Events you create or participate in.
  8. Comment Honors: Recognition received for valuable contributions.
Restricted Information

This data is not shared publicly and is used only for internal administrative purposes:

  1. Mobile Phone Number: For SMS-based account verification.
  2. Quiz Results: Performance data in app quizzes.
  3. Account Verification Status: Verification status of your email or mobile number.
  4. Private Conversations: Messages exchanged with other users or administrators.
Confidential Information

Your password is securely stored and never shared. It is your responsibility to keep it confidential.

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information is shared only when necessary, such as for legal compliance or to address user issues.


Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion. Upon deletion:

  • Your account will be deactivated and labeled as "Deleted User."
  • Certain data, such as forum posts and comments, will be retained for community integrity but anonymized to ensure your privacy.
  • All other personal data will be securely removed from our systems.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we have about you.
  • Correct or update your data.
  • Request account deletion (subject to the conditions outlined above).
  • Express concerns or provide feedback about your data usage.

Security

We prioritize the security of your data but cannot guarantee absolute protection due to the inherent risks of online data transmission and storage. We employ commercially reasonable safeguards to minimize these risks.


Changes To This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes your acknowledgment and agreement to the revised terms.
